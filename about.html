ABOUT THE SCHOOL


Background

The school is a mixed day and boarding Primary School that was founded in 1998 by Mr. and Mrs. Kalemeera using experience they had acquired as head teacher and being in the profession for over 30 years.


The school was formed with the intention of providing quality and all round education to orphan and needy children in the area.
